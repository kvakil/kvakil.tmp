Blogs I Follow (2022)

Tags: meta

Here’s a list of blogs I enjoy. I’m going to keep this list updated throughout 2022. (Randomly ordered.)

  • Philip Zucker’s Blog (RSS): tech blog mostly about functional programming and compilers stuff. Self-described “lab notebook”, so not the best source if you’re looking for highly polished posts. Posts I liked: Making a “MiniKanren” using Z3Py, Translating My Z3 Tutorial to Coq.
  • Emily Short’s Interactive Story (RSS): blog (mostly newsletter nowadays) about Interactive Fiction. Written by Emily Short, who is quite famous in that niche.
  • Talospace (RSS): blog about POWER9/OpenPOWER, mostly hardware and operating system reviews.
  • 100 Rabbits (RSS): “small [primarily tech] artist collective”.
